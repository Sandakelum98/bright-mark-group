Our Process

Our Approach to Quality Cleaning

Our Induction and
Training Programme

Comprehensive Training for Reliable and Effective Cleaning Solutions.

1
Stage 1
  • Hiring Employees
  • According to HR Planning we have identified that 30 more employees are required.
  • Based on the personnel referrals, suitable employees will be selected from an existing applicant pool.
  • If there are no suitable candidates identified, an advertisement may be placed on an Internet Job Listing site.
2
Stage 2
  • BrightMark Group Online Induction and BSCAA
3
Stage 3
  • Hands-on Cleaning Induction provided by the BrightMark Group
  • This is a half-day hands-on training course
4
Stage 4
  • Client OSH Site Induction
  • Airsservices onboarding requirements will be completed.
5
Stage 5
  • On-site Induction
  • Staff are provided site-specific training, including emergency procedures, OSH matters, site access, security, and cleaning schedules.
  • All new staff should work three weeks under the observation of our experienced trained staff member.
6
Stage 6
  • Ongoing Training
  • After three months with the BrightMark Group, all staff are encouraged to complete the 'Cleaning Operation and Asset Maintenance' Qualifications AQF Levels II & III.
