Available Vacancies

Position : Cleaning Supervisor

Location: Perth, WA

Type: Full-Time

BrightMark is seeking a dedicated and experienced Cleaning Supervisor to lead and manage our cleaning teams across various client sites. You will ensure high-quality service delivery, adherence to safety standards, and efficient team operations. This role involves coordinating schedules, inspecting completed tasks, and training staff to meet company standards.

Key Responsibilities

  • Oversee daily cleaning operations across multiple locations.
  • Train and guide cleaning staff to uphold BrightMark’s service excellence.
  • Conduct site inspections to ensure compliance with safety and quality standards.
  • Address client feedback promptly and professionally.
  • Manage supplies and equipment for optimal performance.

Requirements

  • Previous experience in a similar supervisory role.
  • Strong leadership and communication skills.
  • A keen eye for detail and commitment to quality.
  • Valid driver’s license preferred.
