About Us

BrightMark Group

West Australia’s
Best Cleaning Partner

Delivering Reliable, High-Quality Cleaning Solutions Across Sectors Since 2001.

Founded in 2001, BrightMark Group Pty Ltd has grown from a small sole proprietorship into one of West Australia’s trusted names in professional cleaning. Initially established to meet a growing demand, we expanded in 2017 to a proprietary limited company, proudly serving clients across education, government, commercial, and other sectors.

Our team is driven by core values of transparency, accountability, workplace safety, and quality service. West Australian-owned and operated, BrightMark Group is committed to supporting the local economy and building strong relationships with clients. With every project, we strive to provide dependable, high-standard cleaning solutions that our clients can trust.
